# World Bank Data Extraction, Cleaning, and Analysis

## Description
This repository contains an interactive notebook developed to extract, clean, and analyze data from the World Bank. The main objective is to demonstrate the functionality of a pipeline for obtaining subsets of data from the sets available in the World Bank catalog.

## Consulted Indicators
1. SDG #8 Sustainable Economic Development: [Sustainable Development Goals](https://datacatalog.worldbank.org/search/dataset/0037918/Sustainable-Development-Goals)
2. Global Development: [World Development Indicators](https://datacatalog.worldbank.org/search/dataset/0037712/World-Development-Indicators)
3. Governance: [Worldwide Governance Indicators](https://datacatalog.worldbank.org/search/dataset/0038026/Worldwide-Governance-Indicators) *(Selection, monitoring, and replacement of governments, capacity for effective policy formulation, and respect for institutions)*

## Featured Content
- Importing libraries.
- Downloading raw data from the World Bank catalog.
- Cleaning and imputing data, ensuring datasets free of significant NaN values.
- Consolidation and normalization of data to facilitate comparative analysis.
- Statistical analysis, regression models, and principal component analysis.
- Visualization of results and discussion on the relationships between governance indicators and economic development.

## Usage
1. Clone this repository to your local machine.
2. Open the interactive notebook in your preferred Jupyter environment.
3. Follow the step-by-step instructions to execute the pipeline and analyze the results.

Explore, learn, and contribute to the analysis of data related to economic development and governance!
